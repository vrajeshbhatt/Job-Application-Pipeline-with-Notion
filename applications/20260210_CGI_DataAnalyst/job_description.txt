Position Description

At CGI, we are seeking an experienced and driven Data Analyst with strong data engineering and data platform capabilities. In this hybrid role, you will bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions, focusing on data-centric initiatives in the insurance domain. The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience in SQL, ETL processes, and modern Azure-based data platforms (including Databricks and Medallion architecture), along with strong skills in business requirement gathering, stakeholder engagement, and supporting the full project lifecycle. This is a permanent, full-time role based in Atlantic Canada on a hybrid basis (2–3 days in office).

Your future duties and responsibilities

Collaborate with system owners, business users, and data engineering teams to define and document data requirements, operational goals, and analytics needs
Lead requirements-gathering sessions for data-centric projects to improve business processes and information flow
Analyze existing systems and data processes to identify areas for enhancement and optimization
Translate business needs into functional and technical requirements for developers, data engineers, and other technical stakeholders
Support data modeling, data quality, and data governance efforts by providing clear and structured documentation
Create process models, data flow diagrams, use cases, and user stories to communicate solutions
Validate business requirements, support User Acceptance Testing (UAT), and coordinate with QA teams to ensure solution quality
Participate in the design and delivery of ETL workflows, data mappings, and integration logic
Work with Azure Databricks to design and optimize data pipelines following Medallion architecture principles (Bronze, Silver, Gold layers)
Ensure alignment of system solutions with enterprise data architecture and compliance standards
Work across both in-person and virtual teams in a hybrid work environment


Required Qualifications To Be Successful In This Role

4–6 years as a Data Analyst in Insurance Domain

Technical Skills & Tools (Must have) :

Azure Data Fabric, Azure Data Factory, Dataflows,
Medallion Architecture.
Databricks and Synapse
Python and related libraries (e.g., Pandas, PySpark) for ETL and data processing in Databricks
Data Mapping, Lineage, and Metadata management
Power BI / Tableau for reporting and visualization
Strong SQL, ETL/ELT, and cloud data architecture expertise
Experience with semantic models, dataflows, and reusable datasets
Familiarity with Agile methodologies
