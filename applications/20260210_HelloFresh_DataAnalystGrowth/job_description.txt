S'more About The Team

As a Data Analyst, you will be part of a cross-functional team delivering hands-on data assets to improve marketing performance. You will work closely with Data Analysts and Data Engineers to build, maintain, and enhance analytical tools, reports, and dashboards focused on key marketing areas such as conversions, marketing spend, targets, and profitability. The role involves actively supporting self-service analytics by onboarding users, improving dashboard usability, assisting with forecasting and reporting tools that help Marketing teams make better day-to-day decisions.

Lettuce Share What This Role Will Be Responsible For


Own and maintain self-service analytics tools, including user access, documentation, best practices, and continuous improvement
Coordinate and onboard users onto analytics platform, ensuring high adoption, correct usage, and ongoing engagement
Develop solid functional knowledge of internal analytics tools and datasets
Design, build, and support forecasting models for marketing performance, spend optimization, and budgeting processes
Redesign and enhance dashboards and reports to improve usability, clarity
Combine data from multiple sources to create unified, analytics-ready datasets
Support user acceptance testing (UAT) for new tools, dashboards, and data products
Partner closely with stakeholders in the Marketing department to understand requirements, translate them into analytical solutions, and deliver actionable insights
Present analyses, dashboards, and recommendations clearly to non-technical audiences and senior stakeholders
Act as a bridge between data teams and business users, ensuring alignment on definitions, metrics, and expectations


Sound a-peeling? Here's what we're looking for


Advanced SQL skills and strong experience with BI and self-service analytics tools (e.g. Tableau, Looker, Power BI or similar)
Good understanding of marketing analytics concepts, such as performance measurement, budget tracking, ROI, and campaign analysis
Ability to present data-driven insights to non-technical and senior stakeholders, with strong data storytelling skills and experience onboarding, training, and supporting users with varying levels of data literacy
Familiarity with forecasting and analytical modeling, particularly for marketing performance, budgeting, or financial planning
Working knowledge of data warehouses (e.g. BigQuery, Snowflake, Databricks or similar)
Experience with Python for analysis or modeling is a strong plus

Flexible Hybrid Approach

At HelloFresh, we know that flexible work arrangements are essential in enabling you to do your best work, while balancing your personal and life needs. Offering remote work flexibility, along with the opportunity to interact and collaborate in the office are all a part of creating a great employee experience.

To meet these needs, we are pleased to provide Flexible Hybrid work. Flexible Hybrid is a people-first approach that is based on choice, trust, personalization, and empowers teams to choose when and how often they work from the office and work from home, in addition to team days and company days. This means a minimum of 2 days in office per week, with most teams in office between 2-3 days a week.

HelloFresh Canada uses AI-integrated technology to help us process and evaluate applications more efficiently. This includes tools that screen and assess candidate qualifications based on the requirements for this role. While these tools assist our workflow, all final selection decisions are made by our hiring team.
