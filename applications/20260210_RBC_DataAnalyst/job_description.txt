Job Description

What is the opportunity?

The Capital Markets Data Services team is seeking an Associate / Senior Data Analyst to join our Data Reporting function. Data Services ensures Capital Markets data is complete, accurate, and fit-for-purpose to meet business and regulatory requirements.

In this role, you will be the critical link between data and decision-making. You'll be responsible for designing and maintaining dashboards, analyzing complex datasets, and translating business requirements into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making across the organization. You'll design, deliver, and optimize business intelligence solutions to meet business and operational stakeholder requirements, as well as measure data quality health, identify improvement opportunities, and provide executives with actionable insights to drive strategic priorities. This position offers high visibility with leadership and the opportunity to shape how our organization measures and optimizes its data.

What will you do?

Collaborate with stakeholders across Front Office, Operations, Finance, Risk, Technology etc. to understand requirements, propose suggestions based on Capital Markets knowledge and expertise, and deliver integrated solutions
Design, build, and maintain executive dashboards and visualizations in Tableau to provide key business and operational insights and to track data quality metrics, inclusive of materiality measures
Transform complex data patterns into compelling narratives and strategic recommendations that influence decision-making for leadership
Perform analysis on large datasets to identify trends, anomalies, and root causes of data quality issues, translating findings into risk mitigation strategies and operational improvements
Conduct data discovery and profiling to identify appropriate data sources for reporting purposes and write SQL queries to extract and transform data
Apply statistical methods and analytical frameworks to benchmark performance and forecast trends
Build end-to-end reporting solutions including data extraction, transformation, modeling, and visualization using SQL, Tableau, and ETL best practices
Optimize existing reports and data pipelines for performance, accuracy, and scalability
Establish and maintain metadata management practices
Lead forums with senior stakeholders to present insights, gather requirements, and ensure alignment between reporting solutions and business priorities
Create professional presentations and documentation that effectively communicate complex data scenarios to both technical and non-technical audiences
Stay current with industry best practices in business intelligence, data visualization, and performance management
Recommend and implement process enhancements to improve reporting efficiency and data governance maturity
Contribute to bank-wide data initiatives and strategic planning efforts

What do you need to succeed?

Must-have:

Degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, Statistics, Data Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or related field
5+ years of relevant experience in business intelligence, operations, data analysis, or financial services
Advanced proficiency in Tableau for dashboard development and data storytelling
Strong SQL programming skills for complex queries, data extraction, and analysis of large datasets
Proven experience with ETL processes and data modeling techniques
Strong quantitative and problem-solving skills with ability to analyze large data volumes and synthesize insights
Experience applying data visualization principles and dashboard design best practices
Ability to translate business requirements into technical specifications and reporting solutions
Solid understanding of data governance principles, data quality frameworks, and metadata management
Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills, with experience presenting to leadership
Strong attention to detail with ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver under deadlines
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel for data manipulation and PowerPoint for executive presentations

Nice-to-have:

Experience with Python for advanced analytics and automation
Knowledge of Capital Markets products, trading lifecycles, or market/reference data
Familiarity with data science methodologies (machine learning, pattern recognition)
Background in business intelligence, consulting, strategic planning, or process improvement initiatives
Experience with data governance tools or enterprise data management platforms

What's in it for you?

We thrive on the challenge to be our best, progressive thinking to keep growing, and working together to deliver trusted advice to help our clients thrive and communities prosper. We care about each other reaching our potential, making a difference to our communities, and achieving success that is mutual.

A comprehensive Total Rewards Program including bonuses and flexible benefits, competitive compensation, commissions, and stock where applicable
Leaders who support your development through coaching and managing opportunities
Ability to make a difference and lasting impact
Work in a dynamic, collaborative, progressive, and high-performing team
Opportunities to do challenging work
